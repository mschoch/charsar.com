About Me

I am passionate about promoting social enterprises, mission-driven businesses and organizations and philanthropic causes.


I combine my marketing and communication skills with a masters of business administration (MBA), a unique combination that helps me think critically about ways to elevate a brand, align marketing, communications and business goals and put those plans into action. I believe that we build stronger communities when we can help each other live to our fullest potential. I build brand strategies and connections and inspire people to care.
