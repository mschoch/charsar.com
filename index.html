About Me


I live at the intersection of business logic, strategy and creative storytelling. As an MBA graduate, I bring a unique level of critical thinking to the table. I align your MarComm and business goals into one cohesive plan, then I put that plan into action. I’m especially passionate about working with mission-driven companies and organizations and social enterprises—the kind of teams that are out there making communities stronger.

Skills

  • Integrated Marketing & Communications Strategy: Designing and executing comprehensive plans that align communication with core business objectives.
  • Rebranding and Brand Development: Building and developing brand identities for mission-driven organizations and social enterprises.
  • Multimedia Storytelling: Directing photo and video production to create compelling visual narratives that inspire action.
  • Strategic Content Creation: Developing data-informed social media content that drive authentic community connection.
  • Executive Media & Speaker Training: Equipping leaders with the skills to deliver impactful presentations and help them become the faces of the brand.
  • Partnership & Alliance Development: Cultivating strategic relationships to promote shared goals and expand brand reach.
  • Crisis Communications & Consulting: Providing steady, strategic guidance to protect brand reputation during critical moments.
  • Performance Measurement & Reporting: Translating complex data into actionable insights to prove ROI and refine future strategy.
  • High-Impact Media Relations: Securing meaningful coverage and managing public perception through proactive media engagement.
  • Team Leadership & Mentorship: Driving results through effective supervisory management and team empowerment.
